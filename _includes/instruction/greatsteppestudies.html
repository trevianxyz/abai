Seven Facets of the Great Steppe

First President Nazarbayev articulated seven unique historical achievements of the civilization of the Great Steppe which influenced the development of humanity. Seven Facets is the study of the national, cultural and human history of Kazakhstan in all its depth and complexity. The aim is to use scientific and historical facts to objectively understand the Kazakh peoples' role in world history.

Topics:
Horsemanship, Cradle of the Turkic World, Animal Style, Great Silk Road, Golden Man, Metallurgy
