Musicology of Kazakhstan & the Great Steppe

In the Kazakh musical art this process expressed in the formation of new approaches to the study of folklore; revival of traditions; activating the creative exchange with foreign countries; updating of academic music; formation of national specific mass culture. The result is a new vision of the role and place of Kazakh music in world culture and complication of structure of the Kazakhstani musical culture.

Dombra, Akyndar Aitysy, Kyl Kobyz
